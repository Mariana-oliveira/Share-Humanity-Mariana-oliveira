Hunger

Poor nutrition causes nearly half (45%) of deaths in children under five – 3.1 million children each year

Know how to help

Facts

  • More than 821 million people are undernourished
  • The majority of the world’s hungry people live in developing countries, where 12.9% of the population is undernourished.
  • Sub-Saharan Africa is the region with the highest prevalence of hunger, where 237 people are undernourished.
  • 149 million children under 5 years of age (22% of the global under-5 population) are chronically undernourished
  • Up to 80% of food consumed in a large part of the developing world is provided bu 500 million small farms worldwide

This info is provided by United Nations.
