Education

617 million youth worldwide lack basic mathematics and literacy skills

Know how to help

Facts

  • Enrolment in primary education in developing countries has reached 91% but 57 million primary age children remain out of school.
  • Sub-Saharan Africa represents more than half of children that have not enrolled in school.
  • 262 million children remain out of school.
  • 750 million adults still remain illiterate.
  • Two thirds of the illiterate adults are women.
  • 1 out 5 children between 6 adn 17 years are not attending school.

This info is provided by United Nations.

UN Volunteers

The UNV programme contributes to peace and development through volunteerism worldwide.

In 2018 UNV mobilized 1733 young volunteers aged under 29 years. Volunteers have the ability to reach, support and educate young people in a youth-friendly, innovative way.
